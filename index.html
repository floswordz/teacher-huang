Research Interests

My interests mainly focus on biogeography, biodiversity conservation, evolutionary ecology and macroecology, with special regards to diversity patterns of plants, especially for the endemic species. In addition, one of my ongoing topic focused on the influences of ecological and historical factors shaping species assembly patterns and species diversification rates. I have an interest in detecting the relationship of endemic species and their habitats.
